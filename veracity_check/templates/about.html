About Us


VeracityCheck is an AI bot utilizing NLP (Natural Language Processing) and Machine Learning (ML) tailroed to counter the propagation of fake news.

Introduction


In a digital age overwhelmed by a deluge of information, the battle against fake news is more crucial than ever. Our solution is designed to not only curate and process news data but also to discern genuine articles from their deceptive counterparts.


How It Works

Our ML model is fortified with BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers), a state-of-the-art language representation model. BERT's ability to understand the nuances of context equips our system to sift through news articles with unparalleled acuity.

Holistic Data Collection

The journey begins with meticulous data collection and preprocessing. Our system meticulously assembles a diverse array of news articles, creating a comprehensive dataset that serves as the foundation for training our ML model.



Step into a realm where technology stands as a sentinel for factual information, where NLP and ML converge to fortify the walls against the surge of fake news.
